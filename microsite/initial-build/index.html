Using the visual language of shape, form, color and line to create a composition that conveys emotions and ideas rather than representing something in a realistic manner, departure from reality.

What Makes it so Unique?

Abstract art has always stood apart from traditional art forms. Unlike realism, where the goal is to accurately represent the visual world, abstract art breaks free from the constraints of physical representation. It invites viewers to interpret, feel, and experience art on their own terms.
